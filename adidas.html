Adidas has a wide variety of shoes to offer. Here are its top 5.

(in my opinion)


Adios Pro 4

The Pro 4 is the elite racing shoe of Adidas. It is designed for racing only, from anywhere from a 5k race to a marathon. It features Lightstrike Pro foam, with carbon energy rods that span the whole shoe, giving it that extra energy return and propulsion. It can wear down quickly, and it is priced at $250.


Takumi Sen 12

The Sen 12 is a lightweight version of the Evo SL. It is primarly for those shorter races like the mile and the 5k. It can also be used for those key track workouts. Its midsole is comprised of Dual layer lightstrike Pro foam, and energy rods made of glass fiber for energy return. It is priced at $180.


Adizero Evo SL 2

This is Adidas' faster daily trainer. It features a nylon plate and a Lightstrike pro foam, with a rather simple feel. Nonetheless, it is a great shoe targeted towards those tempo runs or track speed sessions and can even handle daily mileage. It is priced at $150.


Adidas Boston 12

This is designed to be a workout shoe, for those track sessions and tempos. It features a mix Lightstrike 2.0 and Lightstrike pro foam, with carbon rods going along the whole shoe's midsole for the extra energy return. It is priced at $160.


Supernova Rise

The Supernova is Adidas' standard daily trainer, built to cover a majority of your mileage. It features a Dreamstrike+ PEBA foam, it offers a stable and cushioned ride. It is priced at $140.
